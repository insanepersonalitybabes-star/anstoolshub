How to Download YouTube Thumbnails

Downloading YouTube video thumbnails is useful for content creators, marketers, bloggers, and anyone who needs the thumbnail image of a YouTube video. ANS Tools makes it completely free and instant — no login, no app, no browser extension needed.

  1. Copy the YouTube video URL from your browser's address bar or from the YouTube app (Share → Copy Link).
  2. Paste the URL into the input box above and click "Get Thumbnails".
  3. Choose your preferred resolution — Max Resolution (1280×720) for the highest quality, or smaller sizes if needed.
  4. Click the download button next to your chosen thumbnail, or right-click the image and choose "Save image as".

Supported YouTube URL Formats

Our tool supports all common YouTube URL formats:

  • Standard: https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=VIDEO_ID
  • Short: https://youtu.be/VIDEO_ID
  • Shorts: https://www.youtube.com/shorts/VIDEO_ID
  • Embed: https://www.youtube.com/embed/VIDEO_ID

Frequently Asked Questions

Is downloading YouTube thumbnails legal?
YouTube thumbnails are publicly accessible images. Downloading them for personal use, reference, or content creation is generally acceptable. However, using someone else's thumbnail commercially without permission may violate copyright. Always give credit to the original creator.
Why is the Max Resolution thumbnail not available?
Max Resolution (maxresdefault.jpg) is only available for videos that have been processed for HD quality. If the max resolution shows an error, try the High Quality (hqdefault.jpg) version which is available for all videos.
What is the best thumbnail size for YouTube?
YouTube recommends 1280×720 pixels (16:9 aspect ratio) as the ideal thumbnail size. This is the "Max Resolution" option in our tool. File size should be under 2MB for upload.
