Resize your images to exact pixel dimensions in seconds. Our free online image resizer supports all common image formats and lets you choose whether to maintain the original aspect ratio or stretch to exact dimensions.

How to Resize an Image

  1. Upload your image file (JPG, PNG, WEBP, etc.)
  2. Enter the desired width and height in pixels
  3. Check "Maintain aspect ratio" to prevent distortion

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the image resizer free?

Yes, completely free. No account or signup required.

Can I maintain the aspect ratio when resizing?

Yes. Enable the 'Maintain aspect ratio' option and only enter one dimension — the other will be calculated automatically to prevent image distortion.

What image formats does the resizer support?

Our image resizer supports JPG and PNG image formats.

Is there a maximum image size?

Yes, the maximum upload size is 16MB. Most photos and graphics are well within this limit.
