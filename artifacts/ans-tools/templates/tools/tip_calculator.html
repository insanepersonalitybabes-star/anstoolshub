The ANS Tools Tip Calculator helps you quickly determine the tip amount for any bill and split it evenly among any number of people. Use the quick-tip buttons for common tip percentages or enter a custom amount.

How to Calculate a Tip

  1. Enter the total bill amount
  2. Click a quick-tip button or enter a custom tip percentage
  3. Enter the number of people splitting the bill
  4. Click "Calculate Tip" to see the breakdown

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the tip calculator free?

Yes, completely free with no account required.

How do I split the bill between people?

Enter the number of people in the "Number of people" field. The calculator will automatically show each person's share of the bill including the tip.

What tip percentage should I leave at a restaurant?

Standard tipping in the US is 15-20% at restaurants. 15% is acceptable, 18% is good service, and 20% or more recognizes excellent service.

Can I enter a custom tip percentage?

Yes. You can type any custom percentage in the tip field instead of using the quick-select buttons.
