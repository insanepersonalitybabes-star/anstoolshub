Frequently Asked Questions

Is the JPG to PNG converter free?

Yes, completely free with no signup or account required.

Why convert JPG to PNG?

PNG supports transparency (alpha channel) while JPG does not. PNG is also lossless, meaning no quality is lost on conversion. Use PNG for logos, graphics, and images that need transparent backgrounds.

Does conversion affect image quality?

Converting JPG to PNG should not reduce quality. PNG is a lossless format so the image data is preserved exactly.
