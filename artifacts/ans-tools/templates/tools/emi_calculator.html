Frequently Asked Questions

What is EMI?

EMI stands for Equated Monthly Installment. It is the fixed payment amount you make to a lender each month until the loan is fully repaid, including both principal and interest.

How is EMI calculated?

EMI is calculated using the formula: EMI = P × r × (1+r)^n / ((1+r)^n - 1), where P is the principal loan amount, r is the monthly interest rate, and n is the number of months.
