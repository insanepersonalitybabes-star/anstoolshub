Frequently Asked Questions

Is the PNG to JPG converter free?

Yes, completely free with no signup or account required.

Why convert PNG to JPG?

JPG files are smaller than PNG for photographs and complex images, making them better for web use and email attachments. JPG is also more universally supported.

What happens to transparent PNG backgrounds?

Since JPG does not support transparency, transparent areas in PNG images are replaced with a white background during conversion.

Does converting PNG to JPG lose quality?

There may be minor quality loss when converting to JPG because JPG uses lossy compression. Higher quality settings minimize this loss.
