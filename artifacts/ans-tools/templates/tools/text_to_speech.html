Text to Speech

Convert any text to audio using your browser's built-in voices.

Uses your browser's Web Speech API. Available voices depend on your operating system and browser.

How to Use Text to Speech

  1. Type or paste your text into the text area
  2. Select a voice from the dropdown
  3. Adjust the speed and pitch sliders
  4. Click Speak to hear the text
  5. Click Stop to stop playback
