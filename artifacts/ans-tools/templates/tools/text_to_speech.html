Text to Speech

Convert any text to audio using your browser's built-in voices.

Uses your browser's Web Speech API. Available voices depend on your operating system and browser.

The ANS Tools Text to Speech converter uses the Web Speech API built into modern browsers to read text aloud. Choose from multiple voices available on your device, adjust the speaking speed and pitch to your preference.

How to Use Text to Speech

  1. Type or paste your text into the text area
  2. Select a voice from the dropdown (voices depend on your device)
  3. Adjust the speed and pitch sliders as desired
  4. Click "Speak" to hear the text read aloud
  5. Click "Stop" to stop playback at any time

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the text to speech tool free?

Yes, completely free. It uses your browser's built-in Web Speech API at no cost.

What voices are available?

Available voices depend on your browser and operating system. Most modern devices offer multiple language voices. Windows and Mac typically have high-quality voices built in.

Does it work on mobile phones?

Yes. Text-to-speech works on iOS Safari and Android Chrome, though voice options may vary by device.

Can I adjust the reading speed?

Yes. Use the speed slider to make the voice read faster (up to 2x) or slower (0.5x) to suit your preference.
