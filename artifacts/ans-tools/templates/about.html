About ANS Tools

Free, fast, and privacy-friendly tools for everyone.

Our Mission

ANS Tools was built with a simple goal: give everyone access to powerful online tools without paywalls, signups, or complicated software. Whether you need to compress an image, calculate your BMI, or generate a QR code, we have you covered.

What We Offer

  • 25+ free online tools across images, PDF, text, calculators, and converters
  • No account required — use any tool instantly
  • Works on desktop, tablet, and mobile devices
  • Files are processed locally or discarded immediately — we do not store your data
  • Clean, distraction-free interface

Privacy

We take privacy seriously. Files you upload for conversion or compression are processed in memory and are not saved to our servers. We use Google Analytics and AdSense for site improvement and monetization. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Contact
