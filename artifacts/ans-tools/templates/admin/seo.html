SEO Optimization Guide

1. Submit Your Sitemap to Google Search Console

Go to Google Search Console → Sitemaps → paste https://anstools.xyz/sitemap.xml → Submit. This tells Google about all your pages and speeds up indexing significantly.

2. Configure Google Analytics

Go to Settings & Ads → enter your GA4 Measurement ID (G-XXXXXXXXXX). This enables traffic tracking so you can see which tools are most popular and where your traffic comes from.

3. Set Up Google AdSense

Apply for an AdSense account at adsense.google.com. Once approved, enter your Publisher ID and Ad Slot IDs in Settings. Ads will automatically appear on your tool pages.

4. On-Page SEO (Already Done)

  • Every tool page has a unique title, meta description, and canonical URL
  • Open Graph and Twitter Card meta tags on every page
  • JSON-LD structured data for rich snippets
  • Breadcrumb navigation on tool pages
  • Related tools section for internal linking
  • How-to and FAQ sections for featured snippet eligibility
